## Cross-call Patterns (synthesised from `/process-call` outputs)

1. **Recall is uniquely universal.** Every call flagged it as a top pain. Billing is painful for *some*, scheduling is painful for *few*. Recall is painful for *all*.
2. **Buyers are not unified.** Principal dentists care about clinical impact; practice managers care about operational stress. Buying committees of 2 people → longer cycles. [Northside + Greenway both displayed this]
3. **Price anchors are low.** Anchor is "what they pay for standalone tools like LocalRecall ($150/mo)." Full PMS add-on framing triggers higher expected pain. We're fighting category gravity on price.
4. **DentalStack is a looming threat.** 2 of 3 interviewees mentioned they've "heard of" DentalStack — meaning US brand equity is leaking into ANZ even before official launch. We have maybe 2 quarters.
