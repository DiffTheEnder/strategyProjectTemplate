# Dentrix Ascend

**Last enriched**: 2025-11-15.

## Overview

Cloud version of Dentrix (legacy on-premise PMS). Owned by Henry Schein. [CONFIRMED]

Dentrix itself is probably the most-installed dental PMS in ANZ — every one of the 3 discovery-call practices mentioned either Dentrix or a Dentrix-compatible workflow. [CONFIRMED from calls]

## Product

- Full PMS — cloud migration story from on-prem Dentrix. [CONFIRMED]
- Roadmap announcement Q4 2025: AI features including "smart recall" and "automated insurance verification," slated for 2026 H2. [SECONDARY — Dentrix product announcement, Oct 2025]
- Existing recall: basic, template-driven, not ML-powered. [CONFIRMED — Dentrix feature docs]

## Pricing

$1,200+ AUD per month for mid-sized practices; higher with tier-based modules. [SECONDARY — quoted to interviewee at Northside]

Expensive. Perceived as expensive. [CONFIRMED — Dr. Anderton: "Switching is the last thing I'd do but also the whole Dentrix thing is getting pricey."]

## GTM

- Direct sales (Henry Schein field reps) + reseller network. [CONFIRMED]
- Large existing installed base — a lot of growth comes from migrating Dentrix on-prem customers to Ascend. [CONFIRMED]
- Henry Schein's distribution is enormous (dental supplies, equipment) — Ascend rides that channel. [INFERENCE]

## Moats

- **Installed base**: biggest in ANZ dental. [CONFIRMED]
- **Data**: customers have years of patient records in Dentrix. Migration is painful and risky. [CONFIRMED from all 3 calls]
- **Henry Schein distribution**: field reps in every region. [CONFIRMED]

## Threats (to DentaSync)

- **LOW currently, MEDIUM in 12-18 months**: the 2026 AI recall feature announcement directly targets our wedge. If Dentrix Ascend ships a good recall automation inside the PMS, dentists will not pay extra for our tool.
- But: Dentrix has historically shipped slowly. The announcement → shipped gap is usually 12-18 months. [INFERENCE from prior Dentrix roadmap → shipping patterns]

## Where they're weak

- **Innovation speed**: PE-owned, slow to ship. [INFERENCE]
- **UX**: interviewees routinely describe Dentrix workflows as "tired", "dated", "takes forever." [CONFIRMED from all 3 calls]
- **Price**: $1,200+ AUD ceiling, not a fit for cost-sensitive 2-4 chair segment. [SECONDARY]
- **Add-on-friendliness**: Dentrix does not make it easy for third-party tools to integrate. We'd need an official partner slot or a reverse-engineered integration. [INFERENCE]

## Strategic implications for DentaSync

If we go recall-only at $400/mo:
- We ride Dentrix's install base by being the recall fix that Dentrix *doesn't* have in 2025/early 2026.
- We need to ship before Dentrix's 2026 H2 announcement becomes reality.
- Our integration story with Dentrix matters — ideally we become an officially-recommended partner.
